PROGRAM

A performance-based off-season system for Wolfpack shot put and discus athletes, built around absolute strength, explosive power, and rotational athleticism.

Shot Put + Discus 4 Lift Days / Week 2–3 Throw Sessions / Week 24 Weeks High School Level
Core Principles
Absolute Strength First Olympic Lifts Heavy Rotational Athleticism Speed of Movement General → Specific Throw to Maintain Technique

Program Overview — 4 Phases

1
Weeks 1–8
General Strength & Movement
Build work capacity, improve movement quality, learn Olympic technique, increase muscle mass.
2
Weeks 9–16
Max Strength
Increase force production through heavy compound lifting while maintaining explosiveness.
3
Weeks 17–20
Power Conversion
Convert strength into speed. Improve rate of force development and rotational explosiveness.
4
Weeks 21–24
Preseason Peak
Maintain strength, maximize explosiveness, reduce fatigue. Peak throwing performance.

Recovery Recommendations

Sleep
  • 8–9 hours/night
  • Most critical recovery tool
  • Non-negotiable for high schoolers
  • Prioritize consistent bedtime
Nutrition
  • ~0.8–1g protein / lb bodyweight
  • Eat to support training volume
  • Don't undereat during off-season
  • Hydrate consistently
Mobility + Deloads
  • Daily: hips, ankles, thoracic, shoulders
  • Deload every 4th–5th week
  • Reduce volume ~40% on deload
  • Keep movement speed high on deloads
